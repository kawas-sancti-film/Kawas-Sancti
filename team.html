Director
Gauthier Le Pévédic

Gauthier Le Pévédic is a researcher and filmmaker dedicated to documentary cinema exploring religious rituals and spiritual practices around the world.

In 2023, he completed Rio Verde within the Shipibo community of the Peruvian Amazon.

In 2024, he collaborated with the Pangcah community in Tafalong, Taiwan, to complete Kawas Sancti.

Head of Post-Production
Florian Thibert

Florian Thibert began his professional path as an industrial electromechanical repairer and self-taught sound system operator before turning to image-making in 2020.

He founded the technical service company Tekhnè – Music & Film, through which he produces and directs documentary projects.

He is a camera operator for France 3 Régions and a member of the image department of the CST (Commission Supérieure Technique de l’Image et du Son).
