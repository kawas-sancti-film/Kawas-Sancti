Gauthier Le Pévédic
Director

From 2023 to 2025, Gauthier Le Pévédic directed several documentaries immersively with religious and spiritual communities. In 2023, he filmed La Règle within the community of the Augustines of the Mercy of Jesus in Dieppe, providing a look at monastic daily life and contemporary religious life. In 2024, he filmed Rio Verde in Roaboya, a Shipibo village in the Peruvian Amazon, exploring local spiritual and ritual dynamics. In 2025, he directed Kawas Sancti within a Pangcah community in Hualien, Taiwan, focusing on Catholic and shamanic religious practices.

Florian Thibert
Head of Post-Production

Initially an industrial electromechanical repairer and self-taught sound system operator, Florian Thibert turned to image in 2020.

He owns a technical service company, "Tekhnè - Music & Film", with which he produces and directs documentaries.

He is also a camera operator for France 3 Régions and a member of the image department of the CST (Commission Supérieure Technique de l'Image et du Son).

His interests include botany, philosophy, and technical arts.
